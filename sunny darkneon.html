About Me

With over 10 years of experience in neon and low-light photography, I specialize in capturing vibrant, glowing moments that pulse with energy. My work blends technical precision with creative experimentation to produce images that electrify the senses.

Based in Tokyo, I travel worldwide to capture urban nightscapes and create stunning neon portraits. When not shooting, I experiment with light painting and teach workshops on night photography techniques.
